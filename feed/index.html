This unique pen holder is crafted using 156 used pens, creatively repurposed to give discarded plastic a second life. Born out of a passion for sustainability and innovation, this model is part of my mission to reduce environmental waste by recycling old pens into functional, beautiful crafts.

I initially planned to expand this idea into a scalable, eco-conscious product line. However, my current method, while effective, is time-consuming and inefficient for mass production. I’m actively researching and experimenting with new techniques and materials to develop a faster, more efficient process—because I believe sustainable design can be practical and impactful.

If you’re passionate about recycling, product design, or environmental innovation and want to collaborate or contribute, feel free to connect with me. Together, we can build solutions that transform waste into wonder.

A classic puzzle meets personalized art in this distinctive set. It features a custom graphic Rubik’s Cube and a signature holder, created to be both a fun challenge and a standout display piece.

The Custom Graphic Rubik’s Cube:
The centerpiece is a standard Rubik’s cube that has been masterfully modified. Instead of solid colors, each face features a curated collection of vibrant, cartoonish graphic stickers. To maintain the puzzle’s solvability while offering a fresh challenge, the stickers on each side share a matching dominant color. This clever design turns the game from simple color-matching into an engaging challenge of aligning whimsical pictures, adding a delightful layer of complexity and visual flair.

The Signature Cube Holder:
Designed to proudly present the cube, the Signature holder is as unique as the puzzle it supports. The base is expertly crafted from hard, thick paper, giving it a sturdy and durable structure. It is then meticulously covered with brilliant green glitter paper and securely assembled with glue, resulting in a dazzling, eye-catching finish. The stand is personalized with the name “SIYAM” in contrasting red glitter, marking it as a true signature piece and the perfect pedestal for the custom cube.
